OSHU Studio – Capturing Your Story with Passion & Creativity in Zurich and Switzerland

Welcome to OSHU Studio, where photography and videography go beyond capturing moments — they tell your story. Based in Zurich, Switzerland, we specialize in business headshots, event photography, wedding photography, and professional videography for individuals and brands looking for high-quality, authentic visual storytelling. Every project we take on is driven by passion, creativity, and a deep commitment to reflecting the unique essence of our clients.

Photography & Videography with a Personal Touch

At OSHU Studio, we believe that every image should tell a story, and every video should evoke emotions. Whether it’s a business portrait, a romantic wedding photoshoot, or an impactful brand video, our goal is to craft visuals that feel personal, authentic, and timeless. We work closely with you to understand your vision, ensuring that the final product is more than just a photo or a video—it’s a meaningful representation of you, your business, or your special moment.

Business Portraits & Corporate Photography in Zurich

In today’s professional world, a strong visual identity is essential. We offer business headshots and corporate photography that convey confidence, professionalism, and approachability. Whether you need a polished portrait for LinkedIn, a corporate website, or a personal branding project, we ensure that your personality shines through in every shot.

Zurich is a hub of innovation, finance, and creativity, making it the perfect backdrop for modern corporate photography. We work with entrepreneurs, executives, and creative professionals to create headshots and branding images that leave a lasting impression.

Wedding & Engagement Photography Across Switzerland

Your wedding day is one of the most important moments of your life, and it deserves to be captured beautifully. At OSHU Studio, we specialize in wedding photography in Zurich and across Switzerland, telling love stories through natural, heartfelt imagery. From intimate elopements to grand wedding celebrations, we document every detail with an artistic and documentary approach.

If you’re planning a Hochzeitsfotoshooting (wedding photoshoot) in Zurich, we know the best locations—from the romantic streets of the old town to breathtaking views by Lake Zurich or the Swiss Alps. Our focus is on capturing genuine emotions, ensuring your wedding photos reflect the true essence of your love story.

For those planning a Hochzeitsantrag (engagement proposal) in Zurich, we also offer discreet and professional proposal photography and videography to capture the magic of the moment when you say “yes.”

Event Photography & Videography – Capturing Unforgettable Moments

From corporate events to private celebrations, our event photography and videography services in Zurich ensure that no special moment goes unnoticed. Whether it’s a conference, a product launch, or a private party, we blend into the background to capture genuine interactions, emotions, and atmosphere.

Our event videography services in Switzerland go beyond simply recording events—we create compelling films that bring the experience to life. Whether you need highlights of a corporate gathering or a full-length event video, we ensure a seamless combination of storytelling and high-quality visuals.

Personal & Fashion Photography in Zurich

Zurich is a vibrant city with a thriving creative scene, making it the perfect location for fashion photography and personal branding photoshoots. At OSHU Studio, we collaborate with models, designers, and influencers to create stunning fashion editorials and personal branding images that stand out. Whether you’re looking for Mode Fotografie (fashion photography) for a magazine or dynamic content for your social media, we bring a unique artistic vision to every shoot.

For professionals and creatives, we also provide Content Creation Zurich services, producing high-quality imagery tailored for social media, websites, and digital marketing campaigns.
